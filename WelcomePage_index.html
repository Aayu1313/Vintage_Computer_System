This is a page all about vintage computers for 8–10 year olds

Made by Holly Whittington, Viktorija, Aayushi and Marianna Santana.

This website is for our IS50004C Website Design Project.

Vintage computers are like old-school toys of the tech world. They're computers people used a long time ago—before laptops and tablets! Imagine a computer that's super boxy, with tiny screens and big, clunky keyboards. Back then, computers could only do simple things like showing text or playing very basic games with stick-like characters.

Think of them like time machines—they give us a peek into what life was like when your parents or grandparents were kids! People who love vintage computers enjoy fixing them and playing the games from those days. It's a fun way to learn about history and see how far we've come with technology!
